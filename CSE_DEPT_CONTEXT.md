# 🧠 ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering is dedicated to nurturing skilled professionals equipped with strong technical knowledge, innovation capabilities, and problem-solving skills.

The department focuses on:
- Industry-relevant learning
- Hands-on experience with modern tools
- Research and innovation
- Strong academic foundation

Students are trained to adapt to rapidly evolving technologies such as:
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cloud Computing
- Cyber Security
- Data Science
- IoT and Edge Computing

---

# 🎯 VISION

To become a centre of excellence for nurturing quality Computer Science & Engineering professionals to cater to the needs of industry and society.

---

# 🎯 MISSION

- To achieve academic excellence through effective teaching methodologies and hands-on learning.
- To inculcate professional behavior, ethical values, and leadership qualities.
- To prepare graduates to be industry-ready with modern technical skills.
- To strengthen Industry-Institute interaction and promote entrepreneurship.

# 🎓 PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSO)

## PSO 1 – Basic Fundamentals
Analyse fundamental knowledge of computer science to solve complex problems and design effective solutions.

Apply modern tools to solve engineering and societal problems and work effectively in teams.
